Northwind Robotics - Support FAQ

Q: What warranty do Northwind Robotics products include?
A: All robots ship with a standard 3-year limited warranty.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email support@northwind.example or call +1-555-0142 (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm PT).

Q: What is the return policy?
A: Hardware can be returned for a full refund within 30 days of delivery via an
RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) request.

Q: Where are Northwind Robotics products manufactured?
A: All units are assembled at Northwind's facility in Dor-on-Sea.
